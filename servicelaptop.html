SERVICES FOR LAPTOP

# PROBLEM SOFTWARE HARDWARE
1 Laptop is slow Could be malware, too many start-up items (Windows) or build up of temporary files Could be hard drive is fragmented or starting to fail, not enough RAM or an old CPU
2 Lapto keeps restarting Could be automatic OS update problem, adware, virus or other malware Could be graphics card, motherboard or network card drivers
3 Keyboard, mouse, printer or other peripherals aren’t working properly Could be driver issues Could be computer port issues or peripheral component issues
4 Peripheral commands interpreted incorrectly Could be driver issues Could be computer port issues or peripheral component issues
5 App command not working Software if it’s happening in only one app Hardware if it’s happening in more than one app
6 Internet is slow Could be an app in background is consuming the bandwidth Could be hard disk failing
7 Downloads take forever Software if it’s happening in only one app Could be hard disk failing
8 Attachments won’t open Could be driver issues Could be computer port issues or peripheral component issues
